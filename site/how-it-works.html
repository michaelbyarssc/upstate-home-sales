For first-time buyers

How buying a manufactured home actually works.

Six steps, plainly explained. Start to keys is typically 8–14 weeks if you own land, 12–20 weeks for Land+Home.

01
Day 1 — week 2

Browse and shortlist.

Filter our 242-home inventory by what you actually care about: budget, beds, square footage. Save favorites without an account. If you're stuck, call — we'll send three options that fit.

02
Week 2 — 3

Tour, in person or virtually.

Most of our homes have 3D walkthroughs. The rest are on our Lexington lot — visit Tuesday–Saturday 9–6. Bring measurements; we'll print floor plans on the spot.

03
Week 2 — 4

Get pre-approved.

We'll connect you with the right lender for your situation — chattel, Land+Home, or FHA. Pre-approval is usually same-day. We don't take a cut from lenders, so we recommend the best fit, not the highest payer.

04
Week 4 — 8

Sign the contract. Site prep begins.

Once your loan's approved, we put your home on hold and start site work in parallel: foundation, utilities, driveway, septic. Most county permits clear in 10–14 business days.

05
Week 8 — 12

Delivery and setup.

Your home is transported in halves (for double-wides) and joined on-site. Setup takes 2–4 days: leveling, marriage line, skirting, tie-downs, exterior steps. We coordinate the inspector.

06
Week 12 — 14

Punch list, then keys.

Walk through with our service tech. Anything not right gets fixed before you sign off. Final inspection passes, you close, you move in. Five-year structural warranty starts ticking.
