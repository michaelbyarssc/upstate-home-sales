The short version.

Jim Whitlock spent twelve years selling for one of the regional manufactured-home chains before deciding he could do it better — and more honestly — on his own. He bought four acres in Lexington, parked three Clayton singlewides on it, and opened in spring of 1998 with one phone line and a hand-painted sign.

For the first decade, the business grew the way Carolina businesses grow: word of mouth, repeat families, the occasional referral from a Realtor who'd run out of lots to show. Jim's rule was that nobody bought a home without first knowing what their land would cost, what their loan would actually look like, and what the all-in number was the day they signed.

Ellie Whitlock joined full-time in 2014 after a stint in product management at a Charlotte software company. She brought spreadsheets, an inventory system, and a website that didn't look like 2003. Jim brought thirty years of factory relationships and the part where you actually look people in the eye.

Today we sell 200+ homes a year across the Carolinas, work with nine manufacturers, and have closed more Land+Home loans than any other dealer south of Columbia for four years running. The handshake hasn't changed. Pretty much everything else has.

What we won't budge on

Three things, written down so you can hold us to them.

Real prices, posted.

Every home on our site has the actual all-in number — home, delivery in zone, setup, skirting, basic steps. No "starting at" theater.

The right loan, not the highest commission.

We have lending relationships, but we don't take rebates from lenders. Whichever program is best for you is what we recommend.

We answer the phone after.

The day after you close, the year after, ten years after. Service is run in-house, not outsourced. Same number.
