The process

How Land + Home works.

01

Tell us what you need.

County, lot size preference, school district, budget. We start with a 30-minute conversation — no commitment.

02

We source land.

We work with three local realtors and have a private database of off-market parcels. Most buyers see options within 7 days.

03

You pick a home.

From our 242-unit inventory or a custom order from a manufacturer. We'll match the home to the lot you've chosen.

04

One loan, one closing.

Real-estate mortgage covers everything — usually our partner Triad or South State. Closes 30–45 days after underwriting.

05

We prep the site.

Foundation, water, sewer/septic, electric, driveway. Our crew or a local subcontractor — your choice.

06

Delivery + setup.

Home arrives, gets set, skirted, and tied down. Punch-list walkthrough. You move in.
