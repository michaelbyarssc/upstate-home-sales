From Columbia.

  1. Take I-26 W from downtown Columbia toward Spartanburg.
  2. Exit 111A for US-1 / SC-6 toward Lexington.
  3. Merge onto US-1 S / Augusta Hwy; continue 2.4 miles.
  4. Lot is on your right, just past Lake Murray Blvd. Big tan sign.
  5. 20 minutes from State House. 12 miles total.

From Greenville / Upstate.

  1. Take I-26 E from Greenville toward Columbia.
  2. Exit 91 for US-378 toward Saluda / Lexington.
  3. Continue on US-378 E for ~14 miles into Lexington.
  4. Right onto Augusta Hwy at the fourth light.
  5. 1 hour 20 minutes from downtown Greenville.
