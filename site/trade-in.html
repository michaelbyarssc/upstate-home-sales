Trade-In Program

Trade up.
We'll handle the rest.

Already in a manufactured home? We'll appraise it, credit the value toward your new home, and handle the title transfer. Most trade-ins close in 6 weeks.

Free 24-hour appraisal

5 questions. We'll send a written appraisal range within 24 hours.

Why trade with us

Three reasons it's easier than selling.

01

You don't show your home.

No open houses, no strangers walking through, no listing photos. We come to you for one inspection.

02

Title transfer, handled.

Manufactured-home titles are notoriously messy. We process the SCDMV transfer and lien release. You sign once.

03

Tax advantages.

SC sales tax is calculated on the difference between trade-in value and new-home price. Real savings — usually $1,500–$3,000.

Typical timeline

Six weeks, start to keys.
