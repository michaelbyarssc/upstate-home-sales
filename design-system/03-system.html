User flows

Primary buyer flow — browse → quote

01 Land

Home

Geo-defaults to user's ZIP. Hero, 6 featured homes near you, 3 collections, trust strip.

02 Browse

Inventory

Filter chips, real prices visible. Sort by price / sqft / newest. Save to favorites.

03 Compare

Detail

Gallery, floor plan, 3D tour, full specs, similar homes. Sticky price + CTA.

04 Capture

Quote modal

Name, ZIP, phone, timeframe, do-you-own-land. 3 fields min, 5 max.

05 Confirm

Thank-you

"We'll respond within 24 hours." Cross-sell: similar homes + financing prefill.

01

02

How It Works

03

FAQ

12 answers: foundations, lifespan, insurance, land requirements, delivery cost.

04

Land+Home

If they don't own land yet — UHS-package option.

05

Inventory

Now they're ready to actually shop.

Dealer flow — lead → close

01

New lead in

Inbox notifies. Lead carries home reference + buyer's qual answers.

02

Triage

Assign to salesperson. Status: New → Contacted → Touring → Quoted → Closed.

03

Quote

Inventory record's base price + markup % computes display price. Email PDF quote.

04

Close

Mark home as Sold; remove from public inventory automatically.

05

Restock

Receive new inventory from manufacturer; bulk-edit pricing for the new batch.

08

Page inventory

Every page Phase 4 will design, with priority and one-line purpose. Tier 1 = ships in v1 hero demo; Tier 2 = ships in v1 full; Tier 3 = post-launch.

Home Tier 1
Geo-personalized hero, featured homes, collections, "How it works" entry, trust strip, manufacturer wall, testimonial, footer.
Inventory grid Tier 1
Filter chips + sticky bar, sort, paginated home cards with real prices, saved-homes affordance, mobile sheet pattern.
Home detail Tier 1
Gallery, floor plan, 3D tour embed, deep specs, sticky price + CTA, similar homes.
Quote modal Tier 1
5-field max lead capture. Triggered from any "Get a price quote" CTA.
Financing Tier 2
Loan types (chattel vs land+home), lender partners, monthly-payment calculator.
Land + Home Tier 2
Explains the UHS package: land sourcing + home + site prep. Lead form.
Trade-in Tier 2
Trade-in valuation form. Differentiates from category.
How it works Tier 2
6-step explainer with illustrations. First-timer trust-builder.
Manufacturers index Tier 2
Logo wall + per-brand pages with brand story and floor-plan filtered view.
Collections Tier 2
Curated browse: Under $150k, Move-in Ready, 3-Bedroom Family, Tiny/Park Model, Premium.
Areas served + county pages Tier 2
SEO doorway pages for SC + NC counties. Same template, county-specific copy.
Delivery Tier 2
What delivery includes, site requirements, typical timeline, photos of delivery in progress.
FAQ Tier 2
12 answers covering foundations, lifespan, insurance, financing, land, warranty, delivery.
About Tier 2
Family origin story, team page, values. Photo-led.
Locations Tier 2
UHS lot(s) — map, hours, address, directions, "what to expect when you visit."
Contact Tier 2
Phone, form, address, hours. Sticky elsewhere; dedicated page lists everything.
Blog index + posts Tier 3
Editorial / SEO content. Empty at launch; grow into.
Careers Tier 3
Open roles + culture. Empty at launch unless hiring.
